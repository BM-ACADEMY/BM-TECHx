Why Most Startups Struggle to Generate Leads

Many startups fail to get consistent enquiries because they lack a proper startup digital marketing strategy. Common issues include:

  • No clear digital strategy

Without a structured process, results become unpredictable. That’s why a system-based approach is essential for sustainable growth.

The 90-Day Lead Generation System by BM TECHX

This lead generation system is designed to help startups build a strong digital foundation, launch effective campaigns, and generate 30+ enquiries per month through a step-by-step process.

Step 1: Business Audit & Automation Setup

Before running ads or campaigns, understanding the business through marketing automation and audits is crucial.

What Happens in This Stage

  • Business audit and market analysis
  • Understanding the target audience
  • Evaluating current digital presence
  • Identifying gaps in the lead journey

Automation Setup Includes

  • Lead forms
  • CRM or lead management tools
  • WhatsApp or email auto-responses
  • Enquiry tracking system

This ensures that every lead is captured, stored, and followed up automatically.

Step 2: Digital Campaign Launch (Ads + Funnel Setup)

Once the foundation is ready, launch structured digital campaigns.

Campaign Setup Includes

  • Google Ads or Meta Ads
  • Dedicated landing pages
  • Lead magnets (free consultation, guide, offer)
  • Clear call-to-action buttons

Running ads without a funnel wastes money. A proper campaign structure converts visitors into genuine leads.

Step 3: Daily Tracking & Performance Reporting

Most startups stop after launching ads — real growth comes from daily monitoring.

Key Metrics Tracked

Number of leads
Cost per lead
Click-through rates
Conversion performance

Data-driven decisions lead to consistent results.

Step 4: Analysis, Optimization & Skill Development

Lead generation is a continuous process, not a one-time task.

This Stage Focuses On

  • Campaign performance analysis
  • Ad and landing page optimization
  • Better audience targeting
  • Improving messaging and creatives

This phase also builds practical digital marketing skills using real data for students and founders.

When this system is followed properly, startups can expect:
